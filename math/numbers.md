
Cardinal number
===============

  * Tells "how many".
  * Known as "counting numbers".
  * Show quantity.

    Eg:
      8 pupies
      14 friends

Ordinal Number
==============

  * Tells the "order of things in a set.

        frist, second, third ...

  * Show rank or position

    Eg:
        3rd fastest
        6th in line


Nominal Number
==============

  * A label or identification

    Eg:
        zip code 3435234
        Jersey number 4
