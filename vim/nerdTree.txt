

NerdTree:

    About preview window:
        The last active window is the preview window
        Type 'go' on NERDTree entry to open it in preview window
        Use '<leader>N' to jump to preview window.

    Open item in preview window (cursor remain in NERDTree):
        'go'

    Jump to preview and open selection in preview window.
        'o'

    Jump to an new vsplit with selected item in new window.
        'gs'
